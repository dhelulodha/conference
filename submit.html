Formatting Guidelines

Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers by the deadline Jul. 25, 2019, for publication in the International Journals and for oral presentation during the conference. All submissions should be written in English with a maximum paper length of seven (7) printed pages including figures without incurring additional page charges (visit here to know more). Papers exceeding 12 pages will not be accepted at ICOAI 2019.

When preparing your final manuscript, please pay also attention to the following:
* If your paper has been prepared using Microsoft Word, please ensure that you have used the most current version which will help reduce word-to-pdf conversion issues such as embedded fonts, bookmarks, etc.
* No page numbers and no headers/footers
* Use non-zero PDF top and bottom margins to help indicate if there are any page numbers.
The templates of Journals are given in the right.
